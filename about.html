Privacy Policy

How Information Is Used

  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested services.
  • To improve the website and services based on user feedback and behavior.
  • To send updates, newsletters, or promotional materials (only if opted-in).
  • To analyze trends and usage patterns for internal purposes.

User Rights

As a user, the following rights regarding personal data are available:

  • Access: The right to request copies of personal information.
  • Rectification: The right to request corrections to any information believed to be inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Erasure: The right to request erasure of personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Restrict Processing: The right to request restriction of processing personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Data Portability: The right to request transfer of collected data to another organization, or directly to the user, under certain conditions.

Cookies

Cookies are small files placed on devices that help enhance the experience on the website. Cookies are used to:

  • Recognize returning users.
  • Understand interactions with content.
  • Improve website performance and usability.

Users can choose to accept or decline cookies through browser settings. Please note that declining cookies may prevent full advantage of the website.

Data Security

Industry-standard security measures are employed to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is entirely secure. Continuous review of security practices is conducted to maintain a high level of data protection.

Data Retention

Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including satisfying legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.
